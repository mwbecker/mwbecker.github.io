Pittsburgh's Most Famous Dessert
Pittsburgh, PA
This cake is ridiculous. Ridiculously amazing on so many levels. Any cake that's named the greatest cake in America has got some street cred. But when you try this thing in all it's almondy glory you understand it on so many levels. Pittsburgh, stand up!
Joe Ariel - CEO, The Marco Polo of Food

You may think you know Pittsburgh after chowin down on a Primanti Bros. sandwich. You may have even washed it all down with some Iron City Beer while watching a Steelers game. Props. But, the locals have been keeping a big toasty secret from us, and its name is the Burnt Almond Torte.

The Huffington Post recently named the Burnt Almond Torte as The Best Cake America Has Ever Made, which, to say the least, caught our attention. This cake is so beloved, its often the choice cake for all milestones, including weddingsa cakes highest honor.

We're not the only ones who love them!

Prantl's Bakery's Burnt Almond Torte was named the "Greatest Cake America Has Ever Made," by the Huffington Post and is a favorite among celebrities like Jane Fonda.
